OVERSABI

Welcome to Oversabi, a revolutionary decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that is dedicated to empowering Africa's future workforce. Our community-driven edutech platform is designed to provide a comprehensive education in the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in today's job market, from finance to software engineering and everything in-between.

White Paper

Oversabi is an online education platform that helps people in Africa learn the skills they need to succeed in today's job market. We offer a wide range of classes and resources, from finance to software engineering, that are designed to keep up with the latest trends and in-demand skills.

Our community-driven approach means that our members can vote on what classes we offer, so we can make sure we're teaching the skills that are most useful to them.

We also offer support services like mentorship, networking, and career counseling to help our members reach their goals. Everyone is welcome to join Oversabi, regardless of their background or financial situation.
